Beaches


Some Beaches for visit

The main town beach „Lolić“ has been awarded a Blue flag for many years

The main town beach „Lolić“ has been awarded a Blue flag for many years which confirms that the beach satisfies the criteria for sea quality and cleanness of the coast as well as the equipment on the beach. It is a pebble beach on the southwest part of the town. The most interesting part of the beach is the middle part. It is sunny and sandy with shallow waters so it is ideal for children. For those who like to hide away from the sun there is plenty of shade under the pine trees. Visitors of any age will find something for themselves – numerous sports and hospitality facilities near the sea. Bars, restaurants, backeries, stands with pancakes and fritters, beach gadgets and souvenirs, fast food and shops will offer a possibility to spend the day on the beach. Different services are available on the beach, like massage, renting scooters, peddal boats, canoes and boats, deck chairs, sunshades and you can also enjoy in water sports like aqua park, slot machine games, volleyball and zumba... There are also changing cabins and showers on the beach, public toilette and parts of the beach adapted for persons with special needs.



Near the town center of Betina there is a pebble-rocky, town beach Bilava.

Near the town center of Betina there is a pebble-rocky, town beach Bilava. This beach is bathed in sun almost all day with only occasional tree that provides shade, and with it there is a view on Betina whose panorama is dominated by the baroque tower of the church of St. Francis of Assisi. The big advantage is parking next to the beach and close to restaurants and a children's playground which is why the beach is suitable for parents with children. Although there is near some trees provided shade, we recommend that you take an umbrella or a different type of protection from the sun if you are planning on a beach to spend most of the time.



Near the town of Tisno, 2km from the town center, there is a pebble beach Jazine

Near the town of Tisno, 2km from the town center, there is a pebble beach Jazine where besides the gravel there are concrete blocks for sunbathing and some part of the beach are sandy. The sea is shallow and warm, so it is ideal for those who like to play games in the shallow, such as picigin, but also for children and the elderly due to the appropriate water intake. In addition, the beach is equipped with public toilet, showers with fresh water and bordered by pine forests that provide enough shade, so you will enjoy this beach all day long. The beach has a number of fun facilities such as shovels, tennis and mini golf. Near the beach there is a sandy volleyball court, so active tourists will enjoy this beach. For those who like relaxing by the sea, it is possible to rent sun loungers and umbrellas. At the very beginning of the beach there is a grocery store and a bar. There is a fast food and beach Bar Sidro where you can refresh and rest from sunbathing and swimming. Close to the beach there is an auto camp, and for all those arriving by car it is important to know that there is parking near the beach.



Podvrška Beach is located 2 km from the center of Murter, and it can be reached on foot, by bicycle or by car.

Podvrška Beach is located 2 km from the center of Murter, and it can be reached on foot, by bicycle or by car. If you come by car, most of the roads are newly paved, but there is still a part of the road that is macadamically near the end. Next to the beach there is a parking lot which you need a daily ticket. This beautiful beach is the most popular among the local population because it still does not know much about her. On the coast there are bigger pebbles and pebbles, and in the sea is a beautiful sand. When the sea is calm this beach looks like a large natural pool due to the turquoise, clean sea and the white, sandy bottom. The beach is ideal for parents with small children, but also for those who do not want too much crowds on the beach. For those who want to enjoy the beach, you can also rent a sun lounger. Close to the beach there is a pine forest that provides, in hot summer days, a great shade. On the beach there is a cafe, a cottage with palaces and walleyes as well as ice cream. Those who want to, can from the beach Podvrške walk through the camp, by the sea, to the beach Slanica. ! Psssst - If you like active vacation and exploration, we suggest you rent a SUP in Murter to enjoy the beauty of Podvrške beach.
