Food and Beverage


Places for food and beverage

BAR SIDRO - Take the most beautiful moments of your vacation with a breathtaking view and a pleasant beach bar ambiance "Sidro".

The beach bar "Sidro" is located in the bay of Tisno in the bay of Jazine. Relax and enjoy the beautiful ambiance with top service and a varied offer. Whether you are having a cold drink, ice cream for your lazy or want to eat something. We are here for you, visit us.



BAR LEOPOLD - In the very center of Murter you will find a cafe bar leopold where you will certainly be drinking a morning coffee.

Located in the very center of Murter, the cafe & cocktail bar Leopold, the place is where you can enjoy good coffee and fresh toast throughout the morning, and if you are for a chill variety, there is a variety of croissants and cakes available. In the evening, this bar turns into a perfect place to relax and enjoy a glass of wine, beer, classic or signature cocktails. This family cafe holds a tradition of 30 years. During that period it experienced several significant changes, but he remained known for the pleasant atmosphere and above all the good coffee that every morning is prepared for you by an experienced bartender, at the same time a coffee shop owner. As part of the Murter Music Street event, you can have fun once a week listening to live music. The cafe has two outside terrace where you can enjoy the sun or get away from the wind, and the good news is that it works throughout the year.



TAVERN JADRAN - FAMILY TRADITION FROM 1919.

The Jadran Tavern, with the longest tradition and distinctive cuisine, is located in very center of Murter. It occupies an old stone house built in traditional dalmatian style, terrace with barbecue grill, overlooking the main square, sea and the port. Our cuisine is traditionally based on the recipes of the island of Murter. Meat and octopus, tuna fish, cooked seafood specialties, clamshell, grilled dishes, original pasta dishes, Dalmatian desserts … but we are still going to keep pace with modern cuisine … The restaurant is open throughout the year, from 08:00 to 24:00. We have 100 seats on the terrace and in the closed part of the tavern. Serves breakfast, ready meals and meals on request. We organize family celebrations and business receptions.



The Piccolo Tavern is located on the Smokvica Island, on the south side of the Kornati National Park in a quiet and unspoiled natural oasis.

The gastronomic offer of the tavern consists of meat specialties and fresh homemade fish, crabs and other sea delicacies from the Adriatic Sea, caught by the hosts themselves, served on Kornati way. There is also olive oil and grapes and figs schnapps, of own production. Some of the specialties are pâtés from the Adriatic tune, Plate "Piccolo", a pond with a Kornati lobster and a fish from a baking oven. Immerse yourself in the warm atmosphere of the tavern and enjoy the delicacies of Dalmatia!
